India is a diverse and vibrant country located in South Asia, known for its rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and rapid technological growth. As the world's most populous democracy, India is home to over 1.4 billion people and boasts a wide range of languages, religions, and traditions. Economically, it is one of the fastest-growing major economies, with strong sectors in IT, pharmaceuticals, manufacturing, and services. India is also renowned for its contributions to science, mathematics, and literature, while continuing to play a key role in global innovation and diplomacy.
